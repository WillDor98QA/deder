Company Purpose & Entrepreneurship Model

Our Purpose

Deder Farms Retail is a family-owned business in the Eastern part of Ghana that produces high-quality pork. We understand the importance of providing healthy pork to the public, so the pigs are raised under ideal and hygienic farming conditions until they reach table size.

While we primarily supply fresh pork to clients on a wholesale basis, we are expanding the farm services to retailing in branded packages and ensuring better pork preservation, even as market demand fluctuates. This has resulted in the launch of a new retail venture, including the establishment of a cold storage facility in a key market area to store and sell packaged pork products.

This initiative will increase market accessibility, reduce waste, and generate a reliable revenue stream.

Mission & Core Values

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide consumers with premium-quality pork products by leveraging self-sourcing, thereby improving the supply chain.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the preferred pork retailer for pork lovers across Ghana who value quality, taste, and ethical sourcing.

Healthy Products

The farm's pork meat is sourced from a very sanitary farm, and the farm's products are 100% organic and safe.

Community Engagement

We encourage local farmers, offer job opportunities, and help Ghana's Eastern Region thrive economically.

Customer Satisfaction

The farm's customers are its main concern, and we are devoted to providing them with exceptional service at all times.
