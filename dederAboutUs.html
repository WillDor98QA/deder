Our Story

Deder Farms began as a small family operation in Ghana's Eastern Region with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality pork products while maintaining ethical and sustainable farming practices. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in Ghana's meat industry while staying true to our core values and family traditions.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our offerings while maintaining the quality and care that has made Deder Farms a household name for pork lovers throughout Ghana.

Our Approach

At Deder Farms, we understand that quality pork begins with quality care. Our pigs are raised in ideal and hygienic farming conditions, ensuring they grow healthy and strong. We believe in treating our animals with respect and providing them with the best possible living conditions.

Our farming practices are sustainable and environmentally friendly, with a focus on reducing waste and maximizing resource efficiency. This commitment to sustainability extends to our packaging and distribution processes as well.

Sustainable Farming

We implement eco-friendly farming practices to minimize environmental impact while maintaining high standards.

Quality Assurance

Every product undergoes strict quality control to ensure it meets our standards before reaching customers.

Community Support

We work closely with local farmers and provide employment opportunities in Ghana's Eastern Region.

Our Journey

2015

The Beginning

Deder Farms was established as a small family pig farm in Ghana's Eastern Region with just a handful of pigs.

2017

Wholesale Operations

We began supplying fresh pork to clients on a wholesale basis, establishing our reputation for quality meat.

2019

Farm Expansion

Expanded our farming facilities and improved our breeding program to meet growing demand.

2021

Retail Launch

Launched our retail venture with branded packaged pork products to serve individual consumers.

2023

Cold Storage Facility

Established a cold storage facility in a key market area to ensure better pork preservation and accessibility.

2025

Looking Ahead

Continuing to innovate and expand our product range while maintaining our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our Values & Commitment

What Drives Us

At Deder Farms, we're guided by a set of core values that influence everything we do. From the way we raise our animals to how we interact with our customers and community, these principles are at the heart of our business:

  • Quality Excellence: We never compromise on the quality of our products.
  • Ethical Practices: We treat our animals with respect and ensure their welfare.
  • Environmental Responsibility: We minimize our environmental footprint through sustainable practices.
  • Community Support: We invest in our local community and economy.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We place our customers at the center of everything we do.
