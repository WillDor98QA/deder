Our Product Offerings

Product Offerings

Deder Farms is committed to providing premium quality pork products sourced from our family-owned farm in the Eastern region of Ghana. Our pigs are raised under ideal and hygienic farming conditions until they reach table size, ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety.

Pork Ribs

Our premium pork ribs are cut to perfection, offering a great balance of meat and flavor. Sourced from our ethically raised pigs, these ribs are ideal for various cooking methods.

  • Farm-to-table freshness
  • Perfect for grilling and slow cooking

Pork Belly

Our pork belly offers a perfect ratio of fat to meat for exceptional flavor. This versatile cut is suitable for a wide range of cooking techniques.

Pork Chops

  • Consistent thickness for even cooking
  • Excellent source of protein

Pork Tenderloin

  • Ideal for roasting or grilling
